Developed a machine learning model using variational autoencoders to identify early signs of stress in plants. This project combined high-resolution near-infrared spectrometry with advanced deep learning techniques to contribute to sustainable agriculture practices.

  • Published: 2022 IEEE Workshop on Metrology for Agriculture and Forestry

Deep Learning for Rapid Powder Analysis

Developed a deep learning approach for rapid analysis of organic powders using near-infrared and derivative spectroscopy. This project introduced innovative pre-processing techniques and demonstrated the effectiveness of convolutional neural networks in predicting powder composition percentages.

  • Technologies: Python, PyTorch, Near-Infrared Spectroscopy, Signal Processing
  • Presented: 1st Italian Workshop on Artificial Intelligence and Applications for Business and Industries (AIABI 2021)
View Paper

Quantitative Prediction of Organic Materials using CNNs and NIR Spectra

Developed a one-dimensional CNN model for simultaneous quantitative prediction of different organic materials in mixtures using their near-infrared spectra. This project demonstrated high accuracy in predicting composition percentages and explored the model's performance on unseen mixtures.

  • Technologies: Python, TensorFlow, Near-Infrared Spectroscopy, CNN
  • Published: 14th International Joint Conference on Biomedical Engineering Systems and Technologies (BIOSTEC 2021)
View Publication

E-Grid: European Grid - A Cooperative Marketplace

Developed as part of the #EUvsVirus hackathon, E-Grid is a collaborative marketplace designed to coordinate actions between industries, small businesses, and Public Administration during emergencies like the COVID-19 crisis. The platform facilitates organized requests, delivery, and management of critical assets, and can serve as a business-to-business platform post-crisis.

  • Technologies: Python, Azure, Web Development
  • Event: #EUvsVirus Hackathon

Key Features:

  • Prioritized request system for Public Administration during emergencies
  • Discussion board for resolving conflicts in resource allocation
  • Interactive map of companies and orders
  • Database for managing product requests and supplies
